About Decoded Lines

Politics moves extremely fast, especially on social media. Fully understanding it means leaving the conversation entirely. You may see a term, leave the app to search for its definition, lose your place, come back, and find three more terms you don't recognize. Then repeat, and forget what you were even reading!

Decoded Lines is a field guide built for that problem. It's a simulated social feed where glossary terms open inline, right beneath the post that uses them. There are no new tabs, meaning no lost context.

Every definition is intentionally neutral, because the goal of this isn't to tell you how to think about American politics. It is to make sure you have the language/tools/context to form an opinion yourself.
